'INTRESTING FACTS ABOUT CAMELS'
* Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
* Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
* Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.

'INTRESTING FACTS ABOUT CICAD'
* The cicadas are a superfamily, the Cicadoidea, of insects in the order Hemiptera (true bugs).
* They are in the suborder Auchenorrhyncha, along with smaller jumping bugs such as leafhoppers and froghoppers.
